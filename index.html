Visual-Inertial Odometry based SLAM for Drones Survey

State estimation is a challenging problem. The problem is compounded since the sensors involved in the production of this system may be expensive and heavy. Since the weight of a traditional unmanned air vehicle (UAV) is tightly constrained, minimizing the weight of that payload can help tremendously. Monocular Visual Inertial (VINS-Mono) solves these engineering challenges by generating a solution to the state estimation problem using only an inertial measurement unit (IMU) and one monocular camera. The system is able to accurately generate poses of robotic systems though only visual and internal inputs. Connected IoT Device Monitoring via Network Traffic Analysis

My current research revolves around tangible privacy and creating tools for people to better visualize and understand their data. For my research, I have created an algorithm that runs on a Raspberry Pi. The Raspberry Pi intercepts network traffic packets transmitted to and from any given device and analyzes them in real-time. The data analyzed is then sent to an abient interface, in this case, an RGB LED. I chose an RGB LED due to the ubiquitous nature of lightbulbs and their relative ease of use to the user. By controlling the LED's brightness, and color I can create a pulsating effect that notifies users how much data is currently transmitted from their devices. StockChain

This project was prepared for my software engineering class at The University of Pittsburgh. This was a semester-long group project which we developed using SCRUM Methodology. This project targets the problem in our modern society where people my age are not saving and investing their money. StockChain revolutionizes stock trading by creating a virtualized stock trading platform for anyone. StockChain allows users to invest virtual money into real stocks and watch their portfolio grow. The primary goal is to provide a simulation capable of showing the performance of various trading strategies. This will allow users to learn the aspects of investing in the stock market such as buying, selling, and shorting various stocks.
